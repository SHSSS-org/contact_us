Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?
We typically respond to all messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.
Can I submit educational resources through this form?
Yes! Please select "Resource Submission" as your subject and include details about the resource you'd like to share with our community.
Do you have a physical office I can visit?
Our organization primarily operates online to keep costs low and resources accessible. However, we do have meeting spaces available by appointment.
How can I get involved with SHSSS Headlines?
We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our community! You can contribute resources, help with verification, or participate in our discussion forums. Select "Partnership" as your subject to learn more.
